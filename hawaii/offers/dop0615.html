DETAILS OF PARTICIPATION

Eligibility.
No one is excluded from visiting our properties or purchasing a timeshare. You must attend a two-hour timeshare sales presentation in order to participate in this promotion and receive the premium items. If you do not meet the qualifications of this promotion or attend presentation, the difference between the special package price and the currently published nightly rate for the applicable resort at that time, plus premium costs and taxes, may be charged to your credit card. Possession of a major international credit card is required. You must present personal identification such as a driver’s license or passport at time of sales presentation.

You may not participate in this promotion if you have an open, incomplete package requiring attendance at a sales presentation, or have participated in a sponsor promotion requiring attendance at a sales presentation: (i) within the last year at the property or properties promoted in this offer, or (ii) within the last six (6) months at any other properties. Hilton Grand Vacations Club or Hilton Club Members, employees of Hilton Grand Vacations, Hilton Worldwide or their affiliates are not eligible. If married or living together, couples must attend sales presentation together; individuals may purchase separately. Offer not valid with any other promotional offer and limited to one package per household. No groups, consisting of more than one couple traveling together, are permitted.

Accommodation Details.
Reservations are on a space-available basis. Savings and available accommodations may vary by season. Oahu: standard hotel room at Hilton Hawaiian Village Waikiki Beach Resort for up to four people (standard value of up to $279/night). Island of Hawaii: standard hotel room for up to four people at the Hilton Waikoloa Village (standard value of up to $219/night). Upgrades may be available at an additional cost.

Package includes accommodations and offered premiums only. All other charges, sales taxes, expenses, parking fees and the cost of travel are your sole responsibility and not included in this special rate. Holiday, high season or weekend rates may apply. Written confirmation required to check-in.

Premiums/Gifts.
Oahu: Guests will receive a $200 Spend a Night on Us certificate, and 5,000 Hilton HHonors™ Bonus Points (valued at $50). Guests will also receive their choice of two tickets for a luau at the Hilton Hawaiian Village Waikiki Beach Resort (valued at $198) or two tickets for a sunset cruise (valued at $166).

Waikoloa: Guests will receive a $200 Spend a Night on Us certificate and 5,000 Hilton HHonors Bonus Points (valued at $50).

HHonors membership and redemption of points are subject to HHonors Terms and Conditions. Please allow six to eight weeks for HHonors points to be deposited into your account.

$200 Spend a Night on Us certificate is good toward up to a $200 rebate on a one-night stay at a domestic Hilton Worldwide brand hotel. See rebate certificate for terms and conditions.

Premiums are awarded after attendance at sales presentation and may not be redeemed for cash.

Reservations.
Full pre-payment using a major credit card is required. This is a non-refundable, nontransferable advance purchase package. A US$19.95 charge applies to each reservation change made up to 30 days before arrival. Should your plans change within 14 days of your scheduled arrival requiring a change of your reservation dates or arrival date, a fee of $199 will be applied to the cost of the package. The above fees do not apply when purchasing additional nights consecutive to an existing reservation. Packages expire 12 months from date of purchase. Packages must be purchased by June 30 July 10, 2015.

Purpose of Promotion.
Promotions requiring attendance at a sales presentation are for the purpose of introducing the benefits of vacation ownership. As a result of state registration requirements, guests may not be eligible to purchase a vacation ownership interest in the project where the sales presentation occurs. Sponsor reserves the right to change this offer prior to purchase without notice. Information gathered through this promotion will be used to solicit timeshare sales.
